## How to Use

- Drag the **Bass**, **Mid**, and **Treble** sliders to adjust gain (−12 to +12 dB)
- The purple curve shows the combined EQ response across 20 Hz – 20 kHz
- Click **Flat** to reset all bands to 0 dB

## What to Observe

- **Bass shelf**: boosts/cuts frequencies below ~200 Hz equally
- **Mid peak**: a resonant bump centered at 1 kHz — notice how it affects nearby frequencies too
- **Treble shelf**: rises toward high frequencies above ~4 kHz
- Combining all three shapes the overall frequency response — this is exactly how audio equalizers work in practice
