## How to Use

- Set the initial voltage, resistance, and capacitance with the sliders
- Press **Start** to animate the discharge
- Watch the voltage curve, current arrow, and energy bar update in real time

## What to Observe

- **Exponential decay**: V(t) = V₀ · e^(−t/τ), where τ = RC
- **Current direction**: current flows out of the capacitor and through the resistor, decreasing as V drops
- **Energy dissipation**: energy stored in C (½CV²) is converted to heat in R
- **Time constant**: after one τ, voltage falls to 36.8% of its initial value; after 5τ it is essentially zero
