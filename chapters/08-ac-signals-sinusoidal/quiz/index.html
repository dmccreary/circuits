    Chapter 8 Quiz — AC Signals and Sinusoidal Waveforms

    Chapter 8 Quiz

    1. In the expression \(v(t) = V_m \sin(\omega t + \phi)\), which parameter represents the peak amplitude?

    A) \(\omega\)   B) \(\phi\)   C) \(V_m\)   D) \(t\)

    2. A sinusoidal voltage has a peak value of \(V_m = 170\) V. What is its RMS value?

    A) 85 V   B) 108 V   C) 120 V   D) 240 V

    3. A sinusoidal signal has a frequency of \(f = 400\) Hz. What is its period \(T\)?

    A) 400 s   B) 0.4 ms   C) 2.5 ms   D) 25 ms

    4. Given \(v_1(t) = V_m \sin(\omega t)\) and \(v_2(t) = V_m \sin(\omega t + 60°)\), which statement is correct?

    A) \(v_1\) leads \(v_2\) by 60°   B) \(v_2\) leads \(v_1\) by 60°   C) The signals are in phase   D) \(v_2\) lags \(v_1\) by 30°

    5. What is the angular frequency \(\omega\) of the US power grid (60 Hz)?

    A) 60 rad/s   B) 120 rad/s   C) \(120\pi\) rad/s   D) \(60\pi\) rad/s

    6. What is the full-cycle average value of a pure, unbiased sinusoidal waveform?

    A) \(V_m/\sqrt{2}\)   B) \(2V_m/\pi\)   C) \(V_m\)   D) Zero

    7. Euler's formula states that \(e^{j\theta}\) equals:

    A) \(\cos\theta - j\sin\theta\)   B) \(j\cos\theta + \sin\theta\)   C) \(\cos\theta + j\sin\theta\)   D) \(\sin\theta + j\cos\theta\)

    8. A signal that satisfies \(x(t) = x(t + T)\) for all \(t\) is called:

    A) Aperiodic   B) Transient   C) Periodic   D) Stochastic

    9. A voltage signal is \(v(t) = 6 + 10\sin(377t)\) V. What is the RMS value of the AC component only?

    A) 6 V   B) 7.07 V   C) 10 V   D) 9.27 V

    10. What is the crest factor of a pure sinusoid?

    A) 1.0   B) \(\sqrt{2}\)   C) 2.0   D) \(\pi/2\)

    Answer Key

    Question Answer Brief Reason
    1 C \(V_m\) is the coefficient of the sine function — the peak value
    2 C \(V_{rms} = V_m/\sqrt{2} = 170/1.414 \approx 120\) V
    3 C \(T = 1/f = 1/400 = 2.5\) ms
    4 B A positive phase offset means \(v_2\) reaches its peak earlier — it leads
    5 C \(\omega = 2\pi f = 2\pi \times 60 = 120\pi \approx 377\) rad/s
    6 D Positive and negative half-cycles cancel; full-cycle average is zero
    7 C Euler's formula: \(e^{j\theta} = \cos\theta + j\sin\theta\)
    8 C This is the definition of a periodic signal
    9 B \(V_{AC,rms} = 10/\sqrt{2} = 7.07\) V
    10 B Crest factor \(= V_m / V_{rms} = V_m / (V_m/\sqrt{2}) = \sqrt{2}\)
