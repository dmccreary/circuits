    Sparky — AI Image Generation Prompts

    Character Overview

    Name: Sparky the Free Electron Role: Warnings & Cautions Color: Orange (#EA580C) Catchphrase: "Zap! Watch out!"

    Base Character Prompt

    Mascot Base Description

    Please generate a set of new png images with a transparent background for our circuits course mascot Sparky the lightbulb. Do NOT use a white, black or checkered background.

    Base Character: Sparky is a friendly anthropomorphic incandescent light bulb mascot, rendered in a warm, polished cartoon style with soft cel-shading, glossy highlights, and a gentle outer glow.

    Body / Bulb: Classic A-shape incandescent bulb forming the head and body as one continuous glass form Glass is a warm, luminous golden-yellow with bright white highlights on the upper-left curve and a subtle rim light Soft, faint sparkle accents around the glass suggesting a gentle inner glow No visible filament through the glass — the face occupies the front

    Face (centered on the bulb): Two large, round, expressive eyes with bright blue irises, crisp black pupils, and a single white catch-light in each eye Small, soft black eyebrows Two small, soft pink circular blush marks on the cheeks Base /

    Screw: Standard Edison screw base below the glass A blue insulator ring just beneath the glass Silver/gray metallic threaded screw section with subtle highlights Small dark contact tip at the bottom

    Arms & Hands: Two short, tubular yellow cartoon arms emerging from the sides of the bulb White gloves (four-fingered cartoon style) with a yellow cuff band

    Stance & Background: Floating slightly above the ground with a soft, elliptical yellow shadow/glow beneath the base Fully transparent background (no white, black, or checkered fill) Optional subtle warm radial glow behind the bulb, kept minimal so transparency is preserved at the edges
